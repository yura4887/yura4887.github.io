  1. Pixels: px

    Osnovnaia i okonchatelnaia edenica izmerenia. Vse znachenia brauzera v itoge pereschitivaet v pixeli

  2. Procents: %

    Procenti vichisllaiutsia ot takogo szhe svoistva u roditilskogo elementa. Krome sleduiushih cluchaev:

    • margin-left, (margin-right, i t.d.)

      Procent beretsia ot SHIRINI poditelskogo blocka, a ne ot ego margin-chototam

    • line-height (visota stroki)

      Procent berietsia ot TEKUSHEGO RAZMERA SHRIFTA, a ne ot line-heght roditelia

    • width/heght (pri posotion: fixed;)

      Procent berietsia ot shirini/visoti okna, a ne ot shirini/visoti roditelia

  3. Otnositelno shrifta: em

    1em - eto tekushi razmer shrifta. T.e. 2em - v x2 bolshe tekushego, a 0.5em v x2 menshe tekushego.

    Tekushi razmer shrifta
    Pervi tekst - razmer shrifta raven = Tekushi shrift *1.6em = 10px * 1.6 = 16px
    Vtoroi tekst - razmer shrifta = 16px * 2 = 32px

  5. Otnositelno okna: vw, vh, vmin, vmax.
